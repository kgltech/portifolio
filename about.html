About Me

Hello — I'm an experienced educator with a deep passion for unlocking student potential.

My journey spans over 10 years, marked heavily by a transformative period teaching in secondary schools in Rwanda. Immersed in vibrant communities, I developed an approach that bridges distinct cultures and focuses acutely on student-centered learning.

There, with limited resources but an abundance of curiosity, my philosophy crystallized: build confidence, encourage critical thinking, and prioritize mastery through empathy.
